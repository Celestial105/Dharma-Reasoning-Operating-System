# 問訊

> [!NOTE] 核心義理
> 在[[天台宗]]的語境下，「問訊」是 terms of addressing, specifically referring to the act of showing reverence and seeking guidance from a teacher or enlightened being. It is often associated with disciples seeking salvation or expressing sincerity and faith towards the Buddha or Bodhisattvas. This gesture signifies a disciple's earnestness in learning and receiving the Dharma.
